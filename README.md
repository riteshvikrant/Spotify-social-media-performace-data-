# Spotify-social-media-performace-data-
Analyzed social media data to uncover engagement trends and deliver actionable strategy insights.

This repository contains an end-to-end analysis of multi-platform social media performance, focused on extracting actionable insights and optimizing engagement strategies. The project covers the complete analytical workflow including data cleaning, transformation, visualization, KPI calculation, and strategic reporting.

Data Preprocessing: Duplicate removal, missing value treatment, standardization of platform names, and formatting of numeric fields.

Engagement Analysis: Calculation of engagement rate, identification of top-performing posts, and pivot-based comparison of content types and platforms.

Platform Insights: Evaluation of follower growth, engagement trends, and ad spend efficiency to determine the highest-impact platform.

Hashtag & Content Strategy: Ranking and performance assessment of hashtags and content types to identify what works best for each platform.

Campaign Effectiveness: ROI evaluation and comparison of engagement uplift during vs. before campaign periods.

Audience Loyalty: Weekly follower growth visualization, moving averages, and correlation analysis to interpret retention behaviour.

Final Presentation: A concise video summary communicating insights, visual findings, and data-backed recommendations.
